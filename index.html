Frequently asked questions

Do you upload my photos?

No. All processing happens in your browser using JavaScript, so your images never leave your device.

Which files are supported?

JPEG and PNG images work best. Selective keep of metadata is supported for JPEG. PNG export strips textual metadata.

Will image quality change?

The app re-encodes images to clean metadata. Quality is set high by default and you can change it in the app.
